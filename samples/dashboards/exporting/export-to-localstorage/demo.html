The demo allows you to convert the Highcharts Dashboards into JSON and create a new dashboard.

For testing, please click on the button "Export Dashboards" button. The JSON with your "Highcharts Dashboards" configuration will be saved in the local storage.

Click on the "Destroy chart" button for removing the current dashboard.

By clicking on "Import Dashboards" button, the new Dashboard will be created, based on configuration, saved in the local storage.
