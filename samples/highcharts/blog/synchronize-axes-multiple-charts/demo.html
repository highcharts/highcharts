Demo of an experimental plugin to synchronize axes and tooltips across charts. Synchronization of two or more axes is achieved by setting identical custom.syncGroup options to each axes. Sync'ing is only allowed between axes of the same type (xAxis or yAxis).

Synchronization of axes will affect both the initial view and the extremes after zooming.

In this demo, the x axes of the two top charts are synced. The two bottom charts are sync'ed by y axes. The zoomType is set up accordingly.
