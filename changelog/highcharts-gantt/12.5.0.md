# Changelog for Highcharts Gantt v12.5.0 (2026-01-12)

- Most changes listed under Highcharts 12.5.0 above also apply to Highcharts Gantt 12.5.0.

## Bug fixes
- Fixed [#22125](https://github.com/highcharts/highcharts/issues/22125), no drag handle was showing for a partially out of range points.
- Fixed [#23605](https://github.com/highcharts/highcharts/issues/23605), incomplete tooltip in x-range and gantt series when the [x2](https://api.highcharts.com/gantt/series.xrange.data.x2) or `end` date was given as a string.
